1. Using Milk.

Milk is a drink that has many benefits for our body, one of which is to maintain the health and beauty of our skin. Substances in milk can be smooth, soften, whiten the skin. Have you ever thought that taking a milk bath in a big tub is all milk? Of course not, actually if you take a milk bath in a big tub there is a mixture of flour and milk as a natural whitening cream.

How to prepare it:

  • Prepare 5 tablespoons of flour and pure milk or sweetened condensed milk.
  • Mix the two ingredients together into a paste in one container Clean your body with a towel.
  • Apply the paste to the whole body evenly. Wait 30 minutes until the scrub dries.
  • Rinse the body with cold water, do this routine and get the results in 1 weeks.

2. Using Papaya and Honey.

A combination of vitamin B1 and vitamin C that makes skin white and smooth. Using papaya and honey can also save your budget for treatment at the salon.

How to make:

  • Prepare 1 half-cooked papaya which has been peeled and cleaned.
  • Blend or blend papaya meat then mix with one tablespoon of honey, stirring evenly.
  • Apply the mixture to the whole body with a gentle massage to improve blood circulation.
  • Wait for about 20 minutes and rinse with water until clean.

3. Using Avocados.

Avocados contain a lot of vitamin A, vitamin C, vitamin E, protein, and other substances that are perfect for our body's skin beauty. Avocados are one of the fruits that are widely used to make beauty products. But if we make it more satisfied and the results are in accordance with our wishes.

How to make :

  • Prepare 3 ripe avocados.
  • Mash or blend until smooth and put into a container, mix with enough water.
  • Apply avocado cream to the whole body, wait until it dries.
  • Rinse with cold water, do this routine every time you take a shower. For maximum results, use it every day for one week.

4. Using White Eggs and Lime.

This mixture is generally used for face masks. This time try to apply to our entire body.

How to make :

  • Mix the egg white and a few drops of fresh lime, shake until frothy and evenly mixed.
  • Apply to the body you want.
  • Let stand for 15-30 minutes until dry, rinse with clean water.
  • Do this regularly.
